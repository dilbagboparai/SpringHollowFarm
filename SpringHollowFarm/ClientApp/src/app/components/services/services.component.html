Services

Spring Hollow offers a variety of services through Briar Cottage LLC. We require riders to fill out a liability release and wear an approved ASTM riding helmet( we have or bring yours). We have an upper weight limit of 225 lbs to ride our horses. ( we have one horse who can carry 275, additional $20 fee)

Horse Boarding There are a very limited number of full board stalls available.
Full board includes:

  • morning and evening feed and hay
  • daily turn-out
  • daily stall clean
  • blanket changes
  • night checks
  • some availability for farm personnel to hold for blacksmith and vet appointments

Concierge services: ( additional charge, limited availability)

  • grooming, tack-up, un-tack and cool down, tack cleaning

Full Board: $550 per month

Trail Ride Lessons

Must be pre-reserved

One hour trail riding lesson, begin with safety briefing and practice in arena followed by guided trail ride through our woods, up and down hills, stream crossings.

$40 apiece for 1-2 people

$35 apiece for 3-5 people
